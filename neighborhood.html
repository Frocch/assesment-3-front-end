East Orlando

This part of Orlando is very relaxed, you won't find any tourists nor tourist atrractions in this part of town. Everyone here is a local, there are very few vacation homes you can find and most of the time at amazing prices compared to anything closer to the parks (Good to know if you ever plan on visiting, an extra 20/25 minute drive could save you a couple hundred bucks). You get the best of both worlds, since you're only 35/40 minutes away from the parks and all the big tourist attractions of Orlando.

There is a lot to do here aswell, being so close to Downtown Orlando there are plenty of amazing restaurants and bars to chose from, and the incredible diversity of options is the best part. No matter what you're in the mood for, you're guaranteed to find something that will take care of the cravings!
